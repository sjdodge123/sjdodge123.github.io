Forged in the trenches, here are my core philosophies

Automation Focus

No task is too small to be automated away. A dev-ops focused approach to automation and deployment means I strive to avoid getting caught on the paperwork or meaningless tasks assiociated with development. Automate early and focus on what matters!

Architecture first

I see the big picture and can break down all of the steps required to deliver a project. I try to solution engineer in ways that won't lock myself into a design sinkhole. I heavily utilize a layered approaches and data driven solutions.

Strategic Allignment

It's important to know where to focus your efforts to deliver the maximum return to the business. Focused feedback driven development can give so much more bang for your buck.
